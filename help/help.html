❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start typing in Sinhala?

Select your preferred keyboard method (Phonetic or Wijesekara) and start typing. The app will automatically convert English letters to Sinhala characters.

What are the different keyboard layouts?

We support Phonetic (type as you speak), Wijesekara (traditional layout), and the Standard Sinhala Computer Keyboard Layout with Normal, Shift, and AltGr states.

How do I install the app on my device?

Look for the "📱 Install App" button or use your browser's install prompt. The app works offline after installation.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes! The app is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices with touch-friendly controls.

How do I track my progress?

Your WPM (Words Per Minute) and accuracy are tracked in real-time. Check the leaderboard to compare with others.
